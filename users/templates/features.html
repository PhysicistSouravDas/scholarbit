Current Features

  • Every user can Log In/Register, and can set unique username. They can customize their profile.
  • Every logged-in user can post unlimited articles. Emojis can also be included in the articles.
  • Users can visit other awesome user's profiles, and can know more about them.
  • Users can comment below every article. Now users also have the option to Edit or Delete their comments.
  • Logged-in users can change their existing password here, or clicking on their username at top-right.
  • If registered user forgot their password, they can reset their password by providing their email via clicking "Forgot Password" on the Log In page.

Upcoming Features

  • Users can Follow other users, and can be Followed by other users.
  • Users can Vote Up/Vote Down any article.
  • Users can connect their Facebook/Google Account, and get their corresponding profile picture from Facebook/Google.
